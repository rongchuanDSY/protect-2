Topic Change Information

Target Audience

This website is designed for teenagers who are entering skate culture, as well as experienced riders who want higher performance. Beginners often find skateboarding demanding — pressure builds quickly to improve fast and stand out. GLIDE takes a different approach: fluid motion matters more than speed. The site also serves advanced skaters who care about subtle mechanics like stiffness, flexibility, material composition, and control under pressure.

Communication Goals

The goal of this website is to present GLIDE as a brand that values smooth movement over forced intensity. The site communicates that progress develops steadily without pressure. It shows that GLIDE balances beginner accessibility with expert expectations within a single identity. The black, red, and white color scheme communicates structure, strength, intensity, and clarity. The overall message is: skateboarding is a performance craft, not a fashion statement.
