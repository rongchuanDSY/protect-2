Products

GLIDE operates through a dual-level product structure. One line offers classic maple decks designed for beginners and casual riders. These boards prioritize durability and affordability without sacrificing reliability. Since many entry-level skaters are budget-conscious, accessibility matters as much as performance.

A second line features advanced composite builds. Lighter and more resilient, these boards support riders who demand precise control and long-term durability. Instead of dividing progression across different brands, GLIDE allows riders to grow within one system — beginning with foundational models and advancing toward high-performance designs.

Deck Collections

Street Decks: Designed for technical tricks, flip tricks, and urban skating. Medium concave with responsive pop.

Park Decks: Wider shapes with mellow concave. Stable platform for transition, bowls, and ramps.

Complete Setups

Fully assembled boards with matched components. No guesswork. Ready to ride out of the box. Perfect for riders who want dependable performance without assembly.

Components

Wheels: High-rebound urethane formulas. Balanced grip and slide for various surfaces.
Trucks: Lightweight alloy construction. Stable turning response. Durable kingpin for repeated grinds.
