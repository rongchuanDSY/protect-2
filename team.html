GLIDE Team

The GLIDE team represents riders who embody precision, control, and progress. These skaters test our products, provide feedback, and push the limits of what GLIDE equipment can do. They skate street, park, and everything in between.

Flow Team

Marcus Chen

Marcus skates street — ledges, rails, and technical flip tricks. Based in Los Angeles, he has been riding GLIDE decks since 2024. His style balances power with precision. Favorite setup: GLIDE Pro Street 8.25" with 54mm Formula 99a wheels.

>

Sofia Rodriguez

Sofia dominates park transition — bowls, pools, and vert. She brings flow and confidence to every line. Her feedback helped shape GLIDE park deck geometry. Favorite setup: GLIDE Park Deck 8.75" with 56mm Formula 92a wheels.

James Okonkwo

James brings raw energy to street skating. Known for technical ledge combinations and consistent landings, he tests GLIDE durability daily. Favorite setup: GLIDE Pro Street 8.38" with GLIDE Light trucks.

Join the Team

GLIDE is always looking for riders who share our values — precision, control, and steady progression. If you skate with intention and want to help develop better products, reach out through our contact page. Include a video link and brief description of your skating background.

Trust shapes every ride. The GLIDE team exists because these riders trust our equipment — and we trust their feedback to make everything better.
